Privacy

At Decision Analytics, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. We respect your right to control how your information is collected and used. This Privacy Statement explains what data we collect from you when you visit our website, how we use it, and what choices you have.

What data do we collect?
When you visit our website, we may collect some or all of the following data:
- Your IP address and browser type
- Your device type and operating system
- Your location and time zone
- Your browsing behavior and preferences on our website
- Your contact details if you fill out a form or request a service from us
- Your feedback and testimonials if you provide them to us

How do we use your data?
We use your data for the following purposes:
- To provide you with the information and services you request from us
- To improve our website functionality and user experience
- To analyze our website performance and traffic
- To communicate with you about our products, services, and promotions
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of use

How do we protect your data?
We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encryption, firewalls, access controls, and other security tools to protect your data. We also limit the access to your data to only those employees, contractors, and partners who need it to perform their duties.

How do we share your data?
We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share your data with:
- Our service providers who help us with hosting, storage, payment processing, analytics, email delivery, and other functions
- Our business partners who offer complementary products or services that may interest you
- Our affiliates who are part of our corporate group
- Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and interests

How long do we keep your data?
We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We may also retain some of your data for backup, archival, or audit purposes. When we no longer need your data, we will delete it or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policy.

What are your rights?
You have the right to:
- Access your data and request a copy of it
- Correct or update your data if it is inaccurate or incomplete
- Delete or erase your data if it is no longer needed or if you withdraw your consent
- Restrict or limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances
- Object to the processing of your data for legitimate interests or direct marketing purposes
- Transfer your data to another service provider if it is technically feasible
- Withdraw your consent at any time if you have given it to us
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you are unhappy with how we handle your data

Changes to this Privacy Statement
We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated version on our website and indicating the date of revision. We encourage you to review this Privacy Statement periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and share your data.

How can you contact us?
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Statement or how we use your data, please contact us at contact@decisionanalytics.ie
