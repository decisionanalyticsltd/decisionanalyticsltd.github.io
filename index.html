Decision Analytics platform

Software that improves your decision making.

There has been a long-term shortage of data analytics skills and capabilities in the marketplace and non-technical decision makers struggle to draw insight from available data.

At Decision Analytics, we are building a web-based decision support tool that empowers decision makers. The core offering is a web application that combines rich external data (provided by partners) with an organisations internal data. A key feature of Decision Analytics is drag and drop modelling tools that allow users to make strategic decisions informed by both internal and external data.

A core value of Decision Analytics is to keep it simple. The accessible insights platform puts analytics in the hands of those who need it most.
